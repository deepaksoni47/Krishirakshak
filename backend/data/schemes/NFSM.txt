SCHEME: National Food Security Mission (NFSM)
TYPE: Crop Productivity Enhancement
MINISTRY: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
LAUNCH YEAR: 2007

DESCRIPTION:
The National Food Security Mission (NFSM) aims to increase production of rice, wheat, pulses, coarse cereals, and nutri-cereals through area expansion, productivity enhancement, and development of farmer-friendly technologies. The scheme provides cluster demonstrations, distribution of improved seeds, and farm-level inputs.

BENEFITS:
- Free or highly subsidized distribution of certified seeds of improved/resistant varieties
- Distribution of disease-resistant high-yielding variety (HYV) seeds
- Demonstration of integrated pest management (IPM) techniques
- Cluster demonstrations of best practices in disease management
- Subsidized soil amendments, bio-fertilizers, and micro-nutrients
- Farm machinery support for weed control and crop protection
- Farmer training on disease identification and management

ELIGIBILITY:
- Farmers growing rice, wheat, pulses, coarse cereals, and nutri-cereals
- Focus districts notified under NFSM are given priority
- Small and marginal farmers receive priority
- No minimum land holding requirement

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Contact the District Agriculture Officer (DAO) in your area
2. Register for seed distribution under NFSM program
3. Attend cluster demonstrations organized by Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs)
4. Apply for subsidized inputs through the DAO

DISEASE RESISTANT SEEDS:
- NFSM provides seeds of varieties with improved disease resistance
- Replacing old susceptible varieties with NFSM seeds can prevent future disease outbreaks
- Blast-resistant rice varieties available through NFSM

BEST SUITED FOR:
- Farmers growing rice, wheat, or pulses facing repeated disease issues
- Farmers wanting to switch to disease-resistant varieties
- Farmers in NFSM focus districts (check with local agriculture office)

KEYWORDS: seeds, disease resistant varieties, crop productivity, rice wheat pulses, IPM, integrated pest management, demonstration, high yielding variety
