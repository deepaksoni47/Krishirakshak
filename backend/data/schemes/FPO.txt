SCHEME: Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) Promotion Scheme
TYPE: Farmer Collective / Market Access
MINISTRY: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare / SFAC
LAUNCH YEAR: 2020 (10,000 FPOs target)

DESCRIPTION:
The government's 10,000 FPO scheme aims to form and promote 10,000 new Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) over 5 years. FPOs are collective bodies of farmers that enable group purchasing of inputs, collective marketing, and access to credit and technology. FPOs provide collective bargaining power to small and marginal farmers.

BENEFITS:
- Equity grant up to INR 15 lakh per FPO for first 3 years
- Credit guarantee coverage up to INR 2 crore per FPO
- Training and handholding by Cluster Based Business Organizations (CBBOs)
- Collective bulk purchase of fungicides, pesticides, fertilizers at lower rates → reduced treatment cost
- Access to better credit terms through collective creditworthiness
- Collective selling at better prices → improved income to cover treatment expenses
- Technology access: drones, precision agriculture equipment

ELIGIBILITY:
- Minimum 300 farmer members to form an FPO
- FPO must register under Companies Act or Cooperative Society Act
- Any category of farmers, including small/marginal farmers

HOW TO JOIN:
1. Contact existing FPOs in your area via SFAC (Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium)
2. If no local FPO: approach state agriculture department to form a new one
3. Existing FPOs: apply for membership and join collective benefits

BENEFITS FOR DISEASE MANAGEMENT:
- FPO members get bulk purchase discounts on fungicides and pesticides
- Shared use of spraying equipment and drones reduces individual cost
- Collective crop insurance negotiation reduces premium costs
- Technical guidance and disease management workshops organized

BEST SUITED FOR:
- Farmers with high treatment costs who could benefit from bulk purchasing
- Small and marginal farmers needing market access and collective strength
- Farmers in remote areas with limited access to quality inputs

KEYWORDS: FPO, farmer collective, group, bulk purchase, market access, cooperative, fungicide bulk, collective insurance, shared equipment
