SCHEME: Agricultural Infrastructure Fund (AIF)
TYPE: Post-Harvest Infrastructure & Finance
MINISTRY: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
LAUNCH YEAR: 2020

DESCRIPTION:
The Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF) provides medium-long term debt financing facility for investment in viable projects for post-harvest management infrastructure and community farming assets. Beneficiaries get loans at subsidized interest rates with 3% interest subvention per annum.

BENEFITS:
- Loans up to INR 2 crore for individuals, up to INR 2 crore per project for cooperatives
- 3% interest subvention on loan amount (effectively 4-7% interest rate)
- Credit guarantee coverage under CGTMSE for loans up to INR 2 crore
- Can be used for: cold storage, custom hiring centres, primary processing units, sorting/grading units, drying units
- 4 years moratorium on principal repayment
- Flexibility to repay over 7 years

ELIGIBILITY:
- Primary Agricultural Credit Societies (PACS)
- Marketing Cooperative Societies
- Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)
- Self Help Groups (SHGs)
- Farmers, joint liability groups
- Multipurpose Cooperative Societies
- Agri-entrepreneurs, startups, and central/state agencies

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Apply at agriinfra.dac.gov.in portal
2. Submit project proposal, business plan, land documents, and financial projections
3. Bank evaluates viability and disburses loan
4. Interest subvention credited to loan account automatically

BEST SUITED FOR:
- Farmer groups needing cold storage after disease losses to prevent further spoilage
- FPOs wanting to set up primary processing units
- Farmers wanting to invest in crop drying units to reduce post-harvest losses

KEYWORDS: infrastructure, storage, cold chain, post-harvest, loan, interest subvention, FPO, cooperative, processing unit
